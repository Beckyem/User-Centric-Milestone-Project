Park Recomendations

The sun is a rare wonder here in Donegal, so when it does pay us a visit, it's hard to decide how to make the most of the weather in a way the whole family can enjoy. Let us make that decision a little easier with these personal favourites.

Oakfield Park

Well kept gardens, mazes, and a train? Where better to spark that imagination that through the fae forest of Oakefield Park! With booking passes to suit the shape of your family, this magical marvel will surely be a day to remember.

Did You Know?

Donegal's Slieve League cliffs are the second tallest in Ireland, but does that include below the sea level? Maybe it's time for a re-evaluation.


Glenveagh National Park

For something closer to the wild heart of Donegal, you can't go wrong with Glenveagh National Park. Let the kids run far and free in this small corner of Ireland's wilderness.

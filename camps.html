Camp Recomendations

Helping children to learn and grow as they have fun is a difficult act to balance, but with the help of these hidden gems, you can juggle that hope a little easier.

Smartsteps Afterschool

Though technically an afterschool service, Smartsteps also offers seasonal activities including Easter, Summer, and Halloween camps. No need to attend the school hosting the event; choose from six wonderful locations and book your kids in today!

Run Over To SmartSteps Afterschool

Designer Minds

This nation-wide team offers young minds the chance to explore their scientific creativity through STEAM (Science, Technology, Engineering, Art & Maths) activities that make learning fun! Book before July 14th from any of their locations, five of which are right here in Donegal.

Hack Over To Designer Minds

Fun Fact!

Learning through play is a crucial way for children to develop essential skills and knowledge. That means that by running around, playing pretend, and making a mess is an essential part of childhood. Go Figure!


Bondoran Surf Co

Sun, sand, sea.. That's the heart of every Summer, so why not give your kids an experience they won't forget? Teach them how to surf safely, right here in Bondoran!

Splash into Bondoran Surf Co

Deane's Farm Equestrian Centre

Got an animal lover in your family? Deane's Farm offers beginner horse lessons from six years up. Perfect for giving in to your child's demand for a pony, without actually doing so.

Trot To Deane's Farm
